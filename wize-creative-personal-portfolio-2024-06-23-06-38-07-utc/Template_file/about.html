Who am I?

I'm a Digital Media graduate (Class of 2025), passionate about digital communication, branding, and creative storytelling. I discovered the emotional power of visual content during my Erasmus experience at the University of Madeira, where I studied Design and Visual Arts.

I'm also a certified lower secondary education teacher and deeply committed to bringing media education closer to children and teenagers through non-formal, inspiring methods that spark curiosity and critical thinking.

When I’m not behind a screen, I enjoy organizing cultural events, volunteering, traveling, collecting vinyl records, and discovering new music.
